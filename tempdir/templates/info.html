Design of a Maturity Checker Device For Coconuts using Deep Learning


The project is a Coconut Maturity Checker device using Deep Learning.
The maturity of the coconut would be based on three criterias which are color, shape, and sound.

Main Objective:
  • The main objective of the project is to design a device that can detect the maturity of the coconut using deep learning that meets the client's needs in compliance with engineering standards and with consideration of the trade-offs based on identified constraints.

Specific Objectives:
  • To design a prototype that can determine the maturity of the coconut;
  • To capture the color, shape, and sound from knocking off the coconut; and
  • To test and evaluate the accuracy and reliability of the prototype.
